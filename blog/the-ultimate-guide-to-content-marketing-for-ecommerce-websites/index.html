Set Free the Old SEO Mindset

Firstly, do not be stuck on a particular SEO tactic, no matter how great it is. Always change your guides and tactics according to time and your audience’s needs. Make yourself flexible and adaptable, as you may end up implementing new content marketing tactics every week for your E-commerce websites. Create Content That User Demands, Not What You Want

Google changes its ranking algorithm time after time. Google mostly focuses on what search users demand, not what the content marketers want. So think from an ordinary person’s perspective, observe if a person searches on google what would it be like. Search for top problems and keywords regarding your service or product, and implement them in your content.

Keep Your Contents Clear and Concise

Whenever we search for a topic or particular information, we find uncountable sources of it. It becomes very puzzling to determine which information is relevant and which is not. So whenever you are trying to write content, make sure it is concise and clear. Users do not have enough time to read a 1200 words content, so try to define your product or service briefly.

Around 60% of users will skip your content if it is lengthy or difficult to understand. You can create more content instead of a single huge post. Make your marketing content short and upload it on different platforms.

Be Active On Different Social Media Platforms 

Social media plays a significant role in product marketing and many more factors. Social media is not only for entertaining us, but we can get uncountable benefits from it. A marketing study shows that social media can boost up the traffic on E-commerce websites if you know how to use it. Many traders are now using social media to advertise and promote their products. Nearly everyone uses social media, so it would be great if you activate your presence on different social media platforms.

Make Your Website Mobile Friendly

We are living in an era where we demand pocket-size gadgets. The more macro gadget, the more advance it is. That is why smartphones can easily beat laptops and computers now. This is why the trend of Android and iPhone app development is highly growing. As they can do nearly all the work of a computer. This implies that you can have a computer in your hands, or perhaps in your pocket. Marketing research shows that around 69% of people use smartphones when they are browsing through the internet. It means that companies should focus more on mobile-friendly websites. A mobile-friendly site is the one which has both computer and mobile algorithm at the backend.

SEO is dynamic, and it changes rapidly, so you should learn about its changes time after time. It is the best way to keep your E-commerce updated. Do Not Use Puzzled Headings, Be Clear

Make your headings clear and understandable in your content. This implies that your headings should not give another meaning to it. In short, do not use double-meaning headings. Google can easily read clear and definite headings and grant your content the rank accordingly. Google reads keywords and also headings when a user searches for a related topic.
